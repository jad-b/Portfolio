****             ****
    Common Errors
====             ====

"'AST_Whatever' does not name a type'"
 - You have not yet declared AST_Whatever within AST.h

"$# of 'BlahSomethingYeah' has no declared type'
- You have not assigned BlahSomethingYeah to a type at the top of parse.yy
  * Don't forget to check the # of $#!
